1. What is the Crop Recommendation System?

  • A Crop Recommendation System is a technology-driven tool designed to assist farmers and agricultural professionals in selecting the most suitable crops for cultivation in a given region or field. It leverages data on soil properties, weather conditions, and other environmental factors to provide scientifically informed recommendations that optimize agricultural productivity and sustainability.

2. How to Use the System

  • Step-by-step guide for using the system::
    • Navigate to the Get recommendations page
    • Enter the fields.
    • CLICK on the Get recommendations

    3. What Data Do You Need?

    • List of user inputs required:
      • Soil composition: Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium levels.
      • pH value.
      • Temperature, rainfall, and humidity.

      4. Tips for Accurate Recommendations
